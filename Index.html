Popular Destinations

Canada

Discover the natural wonders of Canada's stunning landscapes, from the majestic Rocky Mountains to the picturesque lakes of Ontario. Immerse yourself in Canadian culture and hospitality while enjoying outdoor adventures, delicious cuisine, and vibrant cities.

Croatia

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Croatia's coastline and indulge in delicious Mediterranean cuisine on this unforgettable holiday. From charming villages to historic cities, Croatia has something for everyone

New Zealand

Escape to the stunning beauty of New Zealand's pristine landscapes, from the snow-capped Southern Alps to the crystal-clear waters of Milford Sound. Explore Maori culture, indulge in delicious cuisine, and experience the laid-back Kiwi lifestyle on this unforgettable holiday

Activities

Whitewater Rafting - Navigate through rapids and twists and turns of a river on a raft, an exhilarating activity that requires teamwork and quick reflexes.

Zip lining - Soar above the treetops on a zip line, enjoying stunning views and an adrenaline rush as you zip from platform to platform.

Rock climbing - Challenge yourself physically and mentally as you scale cliffs and rocky surfaces, testing your strength and problem-solving skills.

Surfing - Ride the waves and experience the thrill of the ocean, mastering balance and control as you catch the perfect wave.

Hiking and camping - Embark on a multi-day hike, camping in the wilderness and experiencing the beauty of nature up close, a great activity for those who love to explore and connect with the natural world.
